Welcome to the

Wits Division of

Biomedical Informatics



A multifaceted team of Doctors, Scientists, Developers and Researchers dedicated to improving healthcare through technology and research. The team has development electronic healthcare systems for the Wits affiliated hospitals, Charlotte Maxeke Academic Hospital, Helen Joseph Hospital and Chris Hani Baragwanath Academic Hospital. The faculty hosts a web-based research data management tool called REDCap, which is quicker and more secure than paper-based.
