Introductory Workshops



The introductory workshop is a very basic overview of the project design tools available in REDCap. It is intended for those who have never used REDCap before, and need a little bit of extra help to get started. This workshop is held over 1 day and takes you through a step-by-step process of building a simple research data collection tool from start to finish.


Cost:
  • R800 Wits Faculty of Health Sciences staff or student
  • R2400 Non-Faculty member
